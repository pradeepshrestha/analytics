03. Key Performance Indicators

Key Performance Indicators

Businesses need to be able to track how they are performing on key goals or objectives - whether they are growing number of customers, bringing down their costs, increasing revenue on an ongoing basis and myriad others. _ Key Performance Indicators_ or _ KPIs_ are how they measure their success on each of their key business objectives.

KPI Intro V1

Which KPI to use?

The decision regarding which KPI a business analyst should use depends on several factors, including which industry or domain they are working in, which business function they are focusing on, and the type of data they have available to them.
