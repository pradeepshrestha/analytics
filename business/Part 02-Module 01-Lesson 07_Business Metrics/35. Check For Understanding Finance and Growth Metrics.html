Quiz

Which of the following ways is the correct way to calculate ** churn rate**?
SOLUTION: Churn rate is a ratio of the number of customers a business who left at the end of the month compared to the count at the beginning of the month.

We bring in our example of the Smoothie Rocks, the juice bar, for this question. We are interested in knowing the ** Life Time Value of a typical customer who frequents Smoothie Rocks at least once in two weeks**. Which of the following interpretations correctly describes the Life Time Value of the customer for Smoothie Rocks.
SOLUTION: The net value of the customer to the business over the course of time when the customer purchases smoothies from the business.
